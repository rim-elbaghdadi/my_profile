Extracurriculars

I have been invested in extracurricular activities since the beginning of my studies. I joined HeForShe ESSEC in 2019 which is a subsidiary of sorts of HeForShe, solidarity movement for the advancement of gender equality, initiated by the United Nations Women. Our goals are to put an end to gender-based and sexual violence in ESSEC and promote a safe and inclusive learning environment. In my first year at ESSEC, I was elected Secretary General of the association from fall 2019 all throughout 2020, and I remain an active member ever since my mandate. The student organization gained a lot of influence within ESSEC, and we work hand in hand with the administration and different programs’ BDEs (student councils) to set up party angels to all student parties, among other things. I later joined ESSEC Japon in 2021, a student organization promoting the discovery and appreciation of Japanese culture inside ESSEC. I have been acting as Head of Communications since Summer 2022. Most of my tasks consist of managing our social media accounts, designing visuals and acting as artistic director for the association’s projects and events.
